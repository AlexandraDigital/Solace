About Solace

Solace is a peer support platform built on the belief that being heard is the first step toward feeling better. We connect people going through difficult moments with compassionate advisors who offer a non-judgmental space to think things through.

We don't replace professional mental health care — we complement it. Think of us as a first, safe, human step.
